JANE AUSTEN'PRIDE AND PREJUDICE BOOK

Pride and prejuicie is the masterpiece of the english writer Jane Austen, whose blackground is the life of the english bourgeoisie of the early 19th century

The novel shows how relationships motivated by love and money can be promiscuous and petty, covered by the veil of bourgeoisie society.

SUMMARY OF THE NOVEL


Cover of the first edition of Pride and Prejudice (Pride and prejudicie)

The Pride and prejudice plot revolves around Bennet Family, made up married cuple and their five daughters (Jane, Elizabet, Mary, Kitty and Lidya).

The story takes place in a rural area of England in the early 19th century.

Elizabeth Bennet the second daughter is the protagonist of the plot, a bautiful, proud and ypung woman with a strong and avant-garde personality for her time. Lizzie, as her loved ones call Elizabeth, is disturbed by the social conventions of her time.

Her mother, when observing the opinions and attitudes of her daughter, considers that is a lost case regarding her chances of obtaining a husband.

It is important to remember thai in England, at the time in history, the only social role of women was mother and wife, and she had no chance of professional ambitions
